ℹ️ How Commission Works

  • No Commission: Farmers receive 100% of their order item total
  • Percentage: Commission is calculated as a percentage of each order item's total (e.g., 5% of 10,000 FCFA = 500 FCFA commission)
  • Fixed Amount: A fixed amount is deducted from each order item (e.g., 1,000 FCFA per item)

Note: Commission is calculated per order item, not per order. If an order has multiple items from different farmers, each item's commission is calculated separately.
